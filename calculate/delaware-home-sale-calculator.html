About selling in Delaware

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 6.6%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: 1.5-2%; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $360,000 with average days on market near 40. Major markets include Wilmington, Dover, Newark.

Delaware's 6.6% top tax rate on capital gains is partially offset by no sales tax. The state's transfer taxes are among the highest, typically split between buyer and seller.

Market insight: Beach communities like Rehoboth and Bethany see seasonal demand while Wilmington benefits from proximity to Philadelphia and financial industry presence.

Tax note: Delaware has relatively high transfer taxes (1.5-2% of sale price) that can significantly impact net proceeds.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds

  • Confirm eligibility for the 2-year primary residence exclusion ($250k single / $500k married).
  • Track improvements that raise your basis (roof, additions, HVAC).
  • Compare listing agents and negotiate commissions.
  • Address high-impact repairs prior to inspection to avoid large concessions.
