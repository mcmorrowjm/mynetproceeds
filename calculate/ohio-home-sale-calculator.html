About selling in Ohio

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 3.75%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $1 per $1000; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $220,000 with average days on market near 35. Major markets include Columbus, Cleveland, Cincinnati, Toledo.

Ohio's 3.75% top tax rate on capital gains is low for the region. Columbus's growth as a tech hub contrasts with industrial city transitions.

Market insight: Columbus thrives with Ohio State and tech growth. Cincinnati and Cleveland offer value in recovering neighborhoods. Rural areas remain very affordable.

Tax note: Counties and municipalities can add their own transfer taxes to the state rate.
