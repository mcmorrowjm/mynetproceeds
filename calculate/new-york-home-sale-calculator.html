About selling in New York

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 10.9%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: 0.4-0.65%; Typical seller closing costs: 2-3%. Median home price around $450,000 with average days on market near 40. Major markets include New York City, Buffalo, Rochester, Albany.

New York's 10.9% state tax plus NYC's additional 3.876% creates one of the highest tax burdens for home sellers. However, NYC's global market status maintains strong demand.

Market insight: Manhattan remains resilient despite high taxes. Brooklyn continues gentrifying. Upstate offers affordability with Buffalo and Rochester recovering.

Tax note: NYC adds its own taxes plus transfer taxes up to 1.425% for properties over $500,000.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds

  • Confirm eligibility for the 2-year primary residence exclusion ($250k single / $500k married).
  • Track improvements that raise your basis (roof, additions, HVAC).
  • Compare listing agents and negotiate commissions.
  • Address high-impact repairs prior to inspection to avoid large concessions.
