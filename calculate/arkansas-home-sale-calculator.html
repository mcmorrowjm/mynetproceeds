About selling in Arkansas

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 4.7%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $3.30 per $1000; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $200,000 with average days on market near 50. Major markets include Little Rock, Fort Smith, Fayetteville, Springdale.

Arkansas applies a top rate of 4.7% to capital gains, placing it in the middle tier of state taxes. The state's affordable housing market means many sellers see modest but steady appreciation.

Market insight: Northwest Arkansas (Fayetteville-Springdale-Rogers) is booming thanks to Walmart, Tyson, and J.B. Hunt headquarters, while Little Rock offers stability.

Tax note: Arkansas taxes capital gains as ordinary income with rates from 2% to 4.7% based on income levels.
