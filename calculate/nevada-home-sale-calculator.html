About selling in Nevada

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 0%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $1.95 per $500; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $430,000 with average days on market near 35. Major markets include Las Vegas, Henderson, Reno, North Las Vegas.

Nevada's zero state income tax means no state capital gains taxes - a huge advantage saving sellers thousands. Las Vegas and Reno markets have rebounded strongly post-pandemic.

Market insight: Las Vegas continues attracting California refugees seeking tax relief. Reno's tech growth creates Bay Area alternative. Lake Tahoe commands luxury premiums.

Tax note: While no income tax exists, Nevada charges transfer tax of $1.95 per $500 of value.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds
