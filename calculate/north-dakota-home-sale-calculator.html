About selling in North Dakota

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 2.5%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: None; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $260,000 with average days on market near 55. Major markets include Fargo, Bismarck, Grand Forks, Minot.

North Dakota's low 2.5% tax rate on capital gains makes it very tax-friendly. The oil industry's cycles significantly impact western markets.

Market insight: Fargo's diversified economy provides stability. Bismarck benefits from state government. Oil patch areas see volatility with energy prices.

Tax note: One of the lowest tax rates in the nation helps offset remote location challenges.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds

  • Confirm eligibility for the 2-year primary residence exclusion ($250k single / $500k married).
  • Track improvements that raise your basis (roof, additions, HVAC).
  • Compare listing agents and negotiate commissions.
  • Address high-impact repairs prior to inspection to avoid large concessions.
