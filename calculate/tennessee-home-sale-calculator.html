About selling in Tennessee

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 0%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $0.37 per $100; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $320,000 with average days on market near 30. Major markets include Nashville, Memphis, Knoxville, Chattanooga.

Tennessee's lack of state income tax means zero capital gains taxes on home sales. Nashville's explosive growth has created strong seller conditions.

Market insight: Nashville's music and healthcare industries drive demand. Memphis offers affordability. Knoxville benefits from university. Chattanooga attracts remote workers.

Tax note: Transfer tax of $0.37 per $100 is relatively low. No income tax attracts relocating buyers.
