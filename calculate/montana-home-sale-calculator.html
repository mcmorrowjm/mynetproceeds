About selling in Montana

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 5.9%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: None; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $450,000 with average days on market near 50. Major markets include Billings, Missoula, Great Falls, Bozeman.

Montana's 5.9% top tax rate applies to capital gains, but the state's natural beauty and remote work trend have driven significant appreciation, especially in Bozeman and Whitefish.

Market insight: Bozeman's proximity to Yellowstone and ski resorts commands premiums. Remote work has accelerated Montana's population growth.

Tax note: No sales tax helps offset income tax burden. Resort areas see luxury prices despite rural location.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds

  • Confirm eligibility for the 2-year primary residence exclusion ($250k single / $500k married).
  • Track improvements that raise your basis (roof, additions, HVAC).
  • Compare listing agents and negotiate commissions.
  • Address high-impact repairs prior to inspection to avoid large concessions.
