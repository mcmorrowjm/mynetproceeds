About selling in Alaska

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 0%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: Varies by borough; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $350,000 with average days on market near 60. Major markets include Anchorage, Fairbanks, Juneau.

Alaska offers one of the most tax-friendly environments for home sellers with no state income tax or capital gains tax. Your profits from selling Alaska real estate are only subject to federal taxes.

Market insight: The Alaska market is unique with seasonal fluctuations and remote location challenges. Anchorage dominates with 40% of the state's population.

Tax note: No state taxes mean Alaska sellers keep thousands more than sellers in high-tax states like California or New York.
