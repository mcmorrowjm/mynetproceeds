About selling in Minnesota

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 9.85%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $1.65 per $500; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $340,000 with average days on market near 32. Major markets include Minneapolis, St. Paul, Rochester, Duluth.

Minnesota's 9.85% top tax rate on capital gains is among the highest nationally. However, the Twin Cities' diverse economy provides steady appreciation.

Market insight: Minneapolis-St. Paul remains economically diverse with Fortune 500 presence. Rochester benefits from Mayo Clinic while Duluth offers lakefront living.

Tax note: Minnesota's progressive tax system can result in high rates for successful home sales with significant gains.
