About selling in Idaho

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 5.8%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: None; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $450,000 with average days on market near 28. Major markets include Boise, Meridian, Nampa, Idaho Falls.

Idaho's 5.8% flat tax rate applies to capital gains, but the state's explosive growth, especially in Boise, has created strong seller conditions with rapid appreciation.

Market insight: Boise's tech boom and California exodus have driven prices up 100%+ in recent years. Coeur d'Alene attracts luxury buyers seeking mountain lake lifestyle.

Tax note: Idaho offers a flat rate but is considering tax reforms that could lower rates in future years.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds

  • Confirm eligibility for the 2-year primary residence exclusion ($250k single / $500k married).
  • Track improvements that raise your basis (roof, additions, HVAC).
  • Compare listing agents and negotiate commissions.
  • Address high-impact repairs prior to inspection to avoid large concessions.
