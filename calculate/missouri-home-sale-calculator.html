About selling in Missouri

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 4.95%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: Varies by county; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $235,000 with average days on market near 35. Major markets include Kansas City, St. Louis, Springfield, Columbia.

Missouri's 4.95% top tax rate on capital gains is competitive. Both Kansas City and St. Louis offer distinct markets with different opportunities.

Market insight: Kansas City's growth in tech and startups drives demand. St. Louis offers value in historic neighborhoods while Lake of the Ozarks attracts vacation buyers.

Tax note: Missouri is implementing tax reforms to reduce the top rate to 4.5% by 2025.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds
