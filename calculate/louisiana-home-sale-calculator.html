About selling in Louisiana

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 4.25%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: Varies by parish; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $210,000 with average days on market near 55. Major markets include New Orleans, Baton Rouge, Shreveport, Lafayette.

Louisiana's 4.25% top tax rate on capital gains is moderate, though some parishes add additional taxes. New Orleans' unique market dynamics differ greatly from other state regions.

Market insight: New Orleans' historic districts command premiums while facing insurance challenges. Baton Rouge benefits from state government and LSU.

Tax note: Parish-level taxes can add to the state rate, particularly in Orleans Parish (New Orleans).
