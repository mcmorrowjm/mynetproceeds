About selling in Colorado

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 4.4%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $0.01 per $100; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $550,000 with average days on market near 25. Major markets include Denver, Colorado Springs, Aurora, Boulder.

Colorado's flat 4.4% tax on capital gains is competitive, especially given the strong appreciation in Denver and Boulder markets. The state's outdoor lifestyle continues attracting new residents.

Market insight: Denver and Boulder remain seller's markets with limited inventory. Mountain resort areas like Aspen and Vail command luxury premiums.

Tax note: Colorado's flat tax applies to all income types. Recent legislation aims to reduce rates further in coming years.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds

  • Confirm eligibility for the 2-year primary residence exclusion ($250k single / $500k married).
  • Track improvements that raise your basis (roof, additions, HVAC).
  • Compare listing agents and negotiate commissions.
  • Address high-impact repairs prior to inspection to avoid large concessions.
