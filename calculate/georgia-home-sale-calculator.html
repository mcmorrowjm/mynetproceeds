About selling in Georgia

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 5.75%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $1 per $1000; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $350,000 with average days on market near 30. Major markets include Atlanta, Augusta, Savannah, Columbus.

Georgia's 5.75% flat tax on capital gains is moderate compared to neighboring states. Atlanta's booming metro area offers strong appreciation potential, especially in hot neighborhoods like Buckhead and Midtown.

Market insight: Atlanta continues attracting major corporations and film industry growth. Savannah's historic charm and Augusta's stability offer diverse market options.

Tax note: Georgia recently moved to a flat tax rate of 5.75% for all income types, simplifying calculations for home sellers.
