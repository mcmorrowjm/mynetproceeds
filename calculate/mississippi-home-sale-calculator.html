About selling in Mississippi

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 5%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: Minimal; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $180,000 with average days on market near 60. Major markets include Jackson, Gulfport, Southaven, Hattiesburg.

Mississippi's 5% flat tax on capital gains is moderate, while low home prices mean smaller absolute tax bills. The Gulf Coast offers different dynamics than inland areas.

Market insight: Gulf Coast communities like Gulfport and Biloxi attract retirees. Jackson faces challenges while university towns maintain stability.

Tax note: Mississippi's flat 5% rate applies to all income types including capital gains.
