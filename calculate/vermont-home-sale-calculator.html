About selling in Vermont

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 8.75%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: 1.45%; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $380,000 with average days on market near 50. Major markets include Burlington, Montpelier, Rutland, Essex.

Vermont's 8.75% top tax rate on capital gains is high. The state's rural character and vacation home market create unique dynamics.

Market insight: Burlington's lakefront and university drive demand. Ski towns like Stowe command premiums. Rural areas attract remote workers seeking lifestyle.

Tax note: Property transfer tax of 1.45% adds significantly to closing costs.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds

  • Confirm eligibility for the 2-year primary residence exclusion ($250k single / $500k married).

  • Address high-impact repairs prior to inspection to avoid large concessions.
