About selling in New Jersey

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 10.75%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: 0.4-1%; Typical seller closing costs: 1.5-2.5%. Median home price around $480,000 with average days on market near 35. Major markets include Newark, Jersey City, Paterson, Elizabeth.

New Jersey's 10.75% top tax rate on capital gains is among the highest nationally. However, proximity to NYC and Philadelphia maintains strong property values.

Market insight: North Jersey benefits from NYC proximity while Shore communities command seasonal premiums. Princeton area attracts affluent buyers.

Tax note: Combined with high property taxes, New Jersey sellers face significant tax burden despite strong appreciation.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds

  • Confirm eligibility for the 2-year primary residence exclusion ($250k single / $500k married).

  • Compare listing agents and negotiate commissions.
  • Address high-impact repairs prior to inspection to avoid large concessions.
