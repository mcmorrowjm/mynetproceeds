About selling in Arizona

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 2.5%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $2 per deed; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $425,000 with average days on market near 30. Major markets include Phoenix, Tucson, Scottsdale, Mesa.

Arizona's favorable 2.5% capital gains tax rate for 2025 makes it one of the lower-tax states for home sellers. Combined with a hot real estate market in Phoenix and Scottsdale, sellers often see strong returns.

Market insight: Phoenix remains one of the fastest-growing metros in the US, with California transplants driving demand. The luxury market in Scottsdale continues to appreciate.

Tax note: Arizona's flat tax rate of 2.5% applies to all income including capital gains, a significant reduction from previous years.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds

  • Confirm eligibility for the 2-year primary residence exclusion ($250k single / $500k married).

  • Compare listing agents and negotiate commissions.
  • Address high-impact repairs prior to inspection to avoid large concessions.
