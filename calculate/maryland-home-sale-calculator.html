About selling in Maryland

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 5.75%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: 0.5%; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $390,000 with average days on market near 32. Major markets include Baltimore, Columbia, Silver Spring, Germantown.

Maryland's 5.75% state tax on capital gains can be increased by county taxes up to 3.2% additional. Proximity to Washington DC keeps Montgomery County values high.

Market insight: DC suburbs in Montgomery and Prince George's counties remain strong. Baltimore offers urban opportunities while Annapolis attracts naval families.

Tax note: Counties can add up to 3.2% to the state rate, making total taxes as high as 8.95% in some areas.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds
