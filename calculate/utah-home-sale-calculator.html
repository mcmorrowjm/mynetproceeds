About selling in Utah

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 4.65%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: Minimal; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $510,000 with average days on market near 25. Major markets include Salt Lake City, Provo, West Valley City, West Jordan.

Utah's flat 4.65% tax on capital gains is competitive. Salt Lake City's tech boom ('Silicon Slopes') has driven rapid appreciation.

Market insight: Tech growth in Lehi/Provo creates Bay Area alternative. Park City commands ski resort premiums. St. George attracts retirees.

Tax note: Flat tax rate simplifies calculations. Strong economy offsets moderate tax burden.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds

  • Confirm eligibility for the 2-year primary residence exclusion ($250k single / $500k married).
  • Track improvements that raise your basis (roof, additions, HVAC).
  • Compare listing agents and negotiate commissions.
  • Address high-impact repairs prior to inspection to avoid large concessions.
