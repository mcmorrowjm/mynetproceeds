About selling in Kentucky

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 4.5%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $0.50 per $500; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $215,000 with average days on market near 38. Major markets include Louisville, Lexington, Bowling Green, Owensboro.

Kentucky's flat 4.5% tax on capital gains is competitive for the region. Louisville and Lexington offer different market dynamics with bourbon country attracting luxury buyers.

Market insight: Louisville's revival continues with downtown development. Lexington's horse country maintains prestige while Bowling Green grows with manufacturing.

Tax note: Kentucky moved to a flat tax system in 2023, simplifying calculations for sellers.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds
