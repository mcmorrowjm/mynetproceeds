About selling in Virginia

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 5.75%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $3 per $1000; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $380,000 with average days on market near 28. Major markets include Virginia Beach, Norfolk, Richmond, Arlington.

Virginia's 5.75% top tax rate on capital gains is moderate. Northern Virginia's proximity to DC maintains high values despite tax burden.

Market insight: Northern Virginia benefits from federal employment. Richmond offers urban appeal. Hampton Roads serves military families. Charlottesville attracts retirees.

Tax note: Recordation and grantor taxes vary by locality but typically total around $3 per $1000.
