About selling in Oregon

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 9.9%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: Minimal; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $480,000 with average days on market near 32. Major markets include Portland, Eugene, Salem, Bend.

Oregon's 9.9% top tax rate on capital gains is among the highest, though no sales tax provides some offset. Portland's market has cooled but remains expensive.

Market insight: Portland faces challenges but maintains appeal. Bend's outdoor lifestyle commands premiums. Eugene offers college town stability.

Tax note: High income tax rates significantly impact proceeds from appreciated properties.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds

  • Confirm eligibility for the 2-year primary residence exclusion ($250k single / $500k married).
  • Track improvements that raise your basis (roof, additions, HVAC).
  • Compare listing agents and negotiate commissions.
  • Address high-impact repairs prior to inspection to avoid large concessions.
