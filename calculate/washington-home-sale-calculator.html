About selling in Washington

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 7% (gains over $250K); Transfer/Conveyance taxes: 1.28%; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $620,000 with average days on market near 25. Major markets include Seattle, Spokane, Tacoma, Vancouver.

Washington has no income tax, but starting in 2025, capital gains over $250,000 are taxed at 7%. Seattle's tech market has driven massive appreciation.

Market insight: Seattle tech wealth drives prices despite recent cooling. Spokane offers affordability. Bellingham attracts Canadian buyers.

Tax note: New capital gains tax only applies to gains exceeding $250,000. Real estate excise tax of 1.28% applies to all sales.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds

  • Confirm eligibility for the 2-year primary residence exclusion ($250k single / $500k married).
  • Track improvements that raise your basis (roof, additions, HVAC).
  • Compare listing agents and negotiate commissions.
  • Address high-impact repairs prior to inspection to avoid large concessions.
