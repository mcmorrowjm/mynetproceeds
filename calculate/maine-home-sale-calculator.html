About selling in Maine

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 7.15%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $2.20 per $500; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $385,000 with average days on market near 45. Major markets include Portland, Lewiston, Bangor, South Portland.

Maine's 7.15% top tax rate on capital gains is high for New England. Coastal properties have seen significant appreciation while inland areas remain affordable.

Market insight: Portland's food scene and coastal charm drive demand. Summer vacation homes along the coast command seasonal premiums.

Tax note: Maine's transfer tax of $2.20 per $500 is typically split between buyer and seller.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds

  • Confirm eligibility for the 2-year primary residence exclusion ($250k single / $500k married).

  • Compare listing agents and negotiate commissions.
  • Address high-impact repairs prior to inspection to avoid large concessions.
