About selling in Indiana

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 3.05%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: Minimal; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $230,000 with average days on market near 35. Major markets include Indianapolis, Fort Wayne, Evansville, South Bend.

Indiana's low 3.05% flat tax rate makes it one of the more tax-friendly states for home sellers. Indianapolis's steady growth provides consistent appreciation.

Market insight: Indianapolis benefits from diverse economy and central location. College towns like Bloomington and West Lafayette see steady demand.

Tax note: Indiana recently reduced its flat tax rate to 3.05%, among the lowest in the Midwest.
