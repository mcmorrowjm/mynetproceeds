About selling in Texas

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 0%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: None; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $350,000 with average days on market near 28. Major markets include Houston, Dallas, Austin, San Antonio.

Texas has no state income tax, meaning zero capital gains taxes on your home sale - a massive advantage. Combined with strong population growth and corporate relocations, Texas sellers benefit from both tax savings and appreciation.

Market insight: Austin's tech boom continues despite cooling. Dallas-Fort Worth attracts corporate HQs. Houston's energy sector recovers. San Antonio offers affordability.

Tax note: While no income tax exists, Texas has high property taxes averaging 1.8% annually, affecting holding costs.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds

  • Confirm eligibility for the 2-year primary residence exclusion ($250k single / $500k married).
  • Track improvements that raise your basis (roof, additions, HVAC).
  • Compare listing agents and negotiate commissions.
  • Address high-impact repairs prior to inspection to avoid large concessions.
