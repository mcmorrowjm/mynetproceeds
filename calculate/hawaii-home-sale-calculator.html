About selling in Hawaii

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 11%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $0.10-$1.25 per $100; Typical seller closing costs: 1-3%. Median home price around $850,000 with average days on market near 45. Major markets include Honolulu, Pearl City, Hilo, Kailua.

Hawaii's 11% top capital gains tax rate is second only to California, significantly impacting proceeds. However, limited island land supply has driven massive appreciation, especially on Oahu and Maui.

Market insight: Oahu remains the primary market with Honolulu commanding premiums. Neighbor islands offer varying dynamics from luxury resorts to local communities.

Tax note: Hawaii's progressive conveyance tax increases with price, reaching 1.25% for properties over $10 million.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds

  • Confirm eligibility for the 2-year primary residence exclusion ($250k single / $500k married).
  • Track improvements that raise your basis (roof, additions, HVAC).
  • Compare listing agents and negotiate commissions.
  • Address high-impact repairs prior to inspection to avoid large concessions.
