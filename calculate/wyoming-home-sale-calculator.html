About selling in Wyoming

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 0%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: None; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $340,000 with average days on market near 55. Major markets include Cheyenne, Casper, Laramie, Gillette.

Wyoming has no state income tax, meaning zero capital gains taxes on home sales. Jackson Hole's luxury market contrasts with affordable rural areas.

Market insight: Jackson Hole commands ultra-luxury prices. Cheyenne and Casper offer affordability. Energy sector impacts some markets.

Tax note: No income or inheritance taxes make Wyoming attractive for wealthy individuals.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds

  • Confirm eligibility for the 2-year primary residence exclusion ($250k single / $500k married).

  • Compare listing agents and negotiate commissions.
  • Address high-impact repairs prior to inspection to avoid large concessions.
