About selling in Nebraska

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 7.25%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $2.25 per $1000; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $250,000 with average days on market near 35. Major markets include Omaha, Lincoln, Bellevue, Grand Island.

Nebraska's 7.25% top tax rate on capital gains is high for the region. Omaha's steady economy led by Berkshire Hathaway provides stability.

Market insight: Omaha offers Midwest affordability with economic diversity. Lincoln benefits from state government and university presence.

Tax note: Nebraska's documentary stamp tax of $2.25 per $1000 adds to closing costs.
