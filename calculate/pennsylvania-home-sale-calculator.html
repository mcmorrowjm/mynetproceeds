About selling in Pennsylvania

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 3.07%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: 1%; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $260,000 with average days on market near 38. Major markets include Philadelphia, Pittsburgh, Allentown, Erie.

Pennsylvania's flat 3.07% tax on capital gains is among the lowest nationally. Philadelphia and Pittsburgh offer different market dynamics.

Market insight: Philadelphia's neighborhoods vary widely in appreciation. Pittsburgh's tech transformation continues. Pocono Mountains attract NYC buyers.

Tax note: State transfer tax of 1% typically split between parties. Philadelphia adds 3.278% additional transfer tax.
