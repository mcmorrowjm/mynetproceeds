About selling in California

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 13.3%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $1.10 per $1000; Typical seller closing costs: 2-3%. Median home price around $839,200 with average days on market near 21. Major markets include Los Angeles, San Francisco, San Diego, San Jose.

California imposes the highest state capital gains tax in the nation at up to 13.3%, significantly impacting home sale proceeds. However, massive appreciation in markets like the Bay Area and Los Angeles can still result in substantial profits even after taxes.

Market insight: Despite high taxes, California's housing shortage and tech wealth continue driving prices up. Coastal areas command premiums while inland regions offer more affordability.

Tax note: The 13.3% top rate includes a 1% Mental Health Tax on income over $1 million. Long-term gains receive no preferential treatment at the state level.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds

  • Confirm eligibility for the 2-year primary residence exclusion ($250k single / $500k married).
  • Track improvements that raise your basis (roof, additions, HVAC).
  • Compare listing agents and negotiate commissions.
  • Address high-impact repairs prior to inspection to avoid large concessions.
