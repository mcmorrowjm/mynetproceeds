About selling in Iowa

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 5.7%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $0.80 per $500; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $200,000 with average days on market near 42. Major markets include Des Moines, Cedar Rapids, Davenport, Sioux City.

Iowa's 5.7% top rate on capital gains is moderate for the Midwest. The state's stable agricultural economy provides steady if unspectacular appreciation.

Market insight: Des Moines continues growing as an insurance and financial hub. University towns like Iowa City and Ames maintain consistent demand.

Tax note: Iowa is implementing flat tax reforms that will reduce rates to 3.9% by 2026.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds
