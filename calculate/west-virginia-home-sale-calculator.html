About selling in West Virginia

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 6.5%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $1.10 per $500; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $180,000 with average days on market near 60. Major markets include Charleston, Huntington, Morgantown, Parkersburg.

West Virginia's 6.5% top tax rate on capital gains is moderate, though low home values mean smaller tax bills. Economic challenges limit appreciation.

Market insight: Charleston and Morgantown (WVU) show stability. Eastern Panhandle benefits from DC proximity. Rural areas face population decline.

Tax note: Low property values offset higher tax rates for most sellers.
