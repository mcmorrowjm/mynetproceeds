About selling in Illinois

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 4.95%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $0.50 per $500; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $285,000 with average days on market near 38. Major markets include Chicago, Aurora, Rockford, Naperville.

Illinois's flat 4.95% tax on capital gains is moderate, but high property taxes can impact overall returns. Chicago's diverse neighborhoods offer varying appreciation rates.

Market insight: Chicago's north side and suburbs remain strong while south and west sides offer value opportunities. The collar counties continue attracting families.

Tax note: Chicago adds its own transfer tax of $5.25 per $500, significantly higher than the state rate.
