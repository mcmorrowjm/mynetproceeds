About selling in South Dakota

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 0%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $0.50 per $500; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $280,000 with average days on market near 45. Major markets include Sioux Falls, Rapid City, Aberdeen, Brookings.

South Dakota's zero state income tax means no capital gains taxes on home sales. This significant advantage helps offset the state's remote location.

Market insight: Sioux Falls's financial sector drives growth. Rapid City benefits from Black Hills tourism. Agricultural areas see stable, modest appreciation.

Tax note: No income tax makes South Dakota attractive for high-net-worth individuals and retirees.

Tips to maximize your net proceeds

  • Confirm eligibility for the 2-year primary residence exclusion ($250k single / $500k married).
  • Track improvements that raise your basis (roof, additions, HVAC).
  • Compare listing agents and negotiate commissions.
  • Address high-impact repairs prior to inspection to avoid large concessions.
