About selling in New Mexico

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 5.9%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: Minimal; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $320,000 with average days on market near 45. Major markets include Albuquerque, Las Cruces, Santa Fe, Rio Rancho.

New Mexico's 5.9% top tax rate on capital gains is moderate. Santa Fe's art scene and Albuquerque's affordability offer different market dynamics.

Market insight: Santa Fe commands artistic and cultural premiums. Albuquerque offers Southwest living at reasonable prices. Los Alamos benefits from national lab.

Tax note: New Mexico's progressive tax system tops out at 5.9% for high earners.
