About selling in Alabama

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 5%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $0.50 per $500; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $225,000 with average days on market near 45. Major markets include Birmingham, Montgomery, Mobile, Huntsville.

Alabama homeowners face a moderate 5% state capital gains tax on home sale profits. While not as favorable as no-tax states, Alabama's relatively low home prices and steady market make it an affordable place to buy and sell real estate.

Market insight: The Huntsville area continues to see growth due to the aerospace and tech industries, while Birmingham offers stable returns in a recovering market.

Tax note: Alabama allows federal exclusions but taxes remaining gains as ordinary income at rates up to 5%.
