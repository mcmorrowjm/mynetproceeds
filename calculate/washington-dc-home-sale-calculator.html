About selling in Washington DC

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 10.75%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: 1.45%; Typical seller closing costs: 2-3%. Median home price around $680,000 with average days on market near 30. Major markets include Washington.

DC's 10.75% top tax rate on capital gains is among the highest. However, the nation's capital maintains strong demand from government and international buyers.

Market insight: Georgetown and Capitol Hill command premiums. Emerging neighborhoods like Navy Yard show growth. Limited supply maintains prices.

Tax note: Transfer tax of 1.45% for properties over $400,000 adds significant closing costs.
