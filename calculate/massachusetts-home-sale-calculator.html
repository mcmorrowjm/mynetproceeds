About selling in Massachusetts

At a glance: Capital gains tax ~ 5%; Transfer/Conveyance taxes: $4.56 per $1000; Typical seller closing costs: 1-2%. Median home price around $570,000 with average days on market near 28. Major markets include Boston, Worcester, Springfield, Cambridge.

Massachusetts taxes capital gains at 5%, plus a 4% surtax on income over $1 million starting in 2023. Boston's education and biotech sectors drive strong appreciation.

Market insight: Boston and Cambridge command premiums with limited inventory. Western Massachusetts offers affordability while Cape Cod attracts vacation buyers.

Tax note: The new 4% millionaire's tax applies to income over $1 million, potentially raising total rate to 9% for high earners.
